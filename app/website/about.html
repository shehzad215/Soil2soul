About us



What is it all about?

Soil 2 Soul Expeditions is dedicated to providing seekers with life-transforming experiences through spiritually enriching tours by bringing the right scholars.

We are thrilled to introduce a series of immersive tours that delve into the spiritual and cultural richness of India / Bharat. Our tours are designed to provide a profound journey through the essence of India's spirituality, mythology, and heritage. Beginning soon, these tours aim to offer unique experiences to spiritual seekers and explorers.

Our Vision

"At Soil 2 Soul Expeditions, we envision guiding individuals / seekers / travellers on transformative journeys to explore the profound heritage and spirituality of India / Bharat. Our vision is to facilitate meaningful connections, foster personal growth, and create a deep sense of purpose among travellers."

Our Mission

"Our mission at Soil 2 Soul Expeditions is to offer travellers a holistic experience of India's sacred places. We meticulously design itineraries, conduct in-depth research, appoint right scholars to immerse travellers by enabling them to understand historical significance, architectural marvels, and local traditions of sacred places of India / Bharat and Sanatan Dharma."

Our Team

The purpose of our passionate team is to create a unique experience for all the seekers, comprising of subject scholars, researchers, program designers, marketeers, tour coordinators and technology experts to create an immersive spiritual experience.

Reliable

Our reliability comes from years of experience we bring on the table. All our tours are well researched and highly curated to provide an assured experience to all the travellers.

Passionate

Our passion to create transformative journeys of scared India / Bharat is unparallel and our team is always on lookout to curate unique programs that can lead to greater self-awareness and more profound appreciation of life.

Authentic

All our scholars work on collating authentic information and translating into programs that encourages inner growth, mindfulness and deeper connection with the present moment.
   

Spiritual Tours in India

India is known all over the world for its deeply immersive spiritual experiences which can prove to be potentially life changing for some individuals. Going on a pilgrimage tour in India is one of the most thought provoking and a completely cleansing experience for your mind, body and soul. There are innumerable pilgrimage yatras in India which are focused on various different aspects of spiritualism. It is one of the surest ways of having some enlightening experiences. From the snow clad mountain peaks of the north to the glimmering shores of the south India is peppered throughout with some of the most beautiful and thought inducing holy sites.
