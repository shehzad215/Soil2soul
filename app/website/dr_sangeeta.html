Our Team



Dr. Sangeeta Sharma

Dr. Sangeeta Sharma is an esteemed Sanskrit scholar and educator, deeply involved in promoting Sanskrit through teaching, research, and yoga. Her work includes being a Yoga Consultant, Storyteller for NCERT, and leveraging AI for Sanskrit text preservation. She collaborated globally on Sanskrit education with Language Curry and conducts live sessions under PM E-Vidya Yojana.

An expert in stress management and yogic psychology, she has developed educational content for IGNOU and Bhartiya Siksha Board, and provided teacher training. Dr. Sharma coordinated significant NCERT and JMI programs and presented papers at national and international conferences, emphasizing women's roles in peace and yoga's philosophical aspects. Current she is working in Jamia millia Islamia, Delhi as a faculty of Sanskrit.
